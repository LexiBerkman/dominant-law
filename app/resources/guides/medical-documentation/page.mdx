## Build a reliable documentation system

A consistent system improves clarity and reduces avoidable confusion during claim evaluation.

## Core records to maintain

### Clinical records

- Visit summaries and imaging reports.
- Diagnoses and treatment plans.
- Medication and referral details.

### Practical records

- Out-of-pocket expenses.
- Transportation and visit logs.
- Work-impact notes.

## Communication consistency

Keep provider and insurer communication factual and aligned with your documented timeline.

### Do

- Keep dates accurate.
- Use one master timeline.
- Save all written communications.

### Do not

- Guess about long-term outcomes.
- Edit records after the fact.
- Share sensitive details in unsecured channels.

## Georgia note

Claim timelines and legal standards can be short and context-specific. Verify current law before relying on generalized guidance.
