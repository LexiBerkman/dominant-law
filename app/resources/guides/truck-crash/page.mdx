## Why truck crash claims are different

Commercial collision matters can involve multiple responsible parties and technical record sets, including logs, maintenance, and dispatch-related evidence. If the collision also involves pressure from insurer outreach, pair this page with our [insurance adjuster communication guide](/resources/guides/insurance-adjusters).

## Evidence preservation priorities

Early preservation can matter because some operational records are not retained indefinitely.

### High-value records

- Scene photos and vehicle damage documentation.
- Carrier and driver identifiers.
- Report numbers and witness contacts.
- Medical timeline records.

## Insurance and communication strategy

Insurer and defense contact may begin quickly. Keep communications factual and documented.

### Do

- Request claim details in writing.
- Keep a communication log.
- Preserve all letters and emails.

### Do not

- Offer speculative conclusions.
- Share unnecessary details publicly.
- Sign broad releases without review.

## Georgia note

Timelines can be short and legal standards can change. Verify current law before relying on generalized summaries, and use our [medical documentation tips](/resources/guides/medical-documentation) to keep treatment records organized while the claim develops.
