## Communication posture that protects your position

Professional, concise communication can reduce inconsistencies and unnecessary disputes.

## Documentation discipline

Treat communication logs as a core case record.

### What to log after each contact

- Date and time.
- Person and organization.
- Claim number or reference.
- What was asked.
- What you agreed to send next.

## Dos and do nots

### Do

- Stay factual and calm.
- Confirm details in writing.
- Preserve all communications.

### Do not

- Guess about injuries or fault allocation.
- Volunteer unnecessary details.
- Sign releases without review.

## Georgia note

Timelines and legal requirements may be short and fact-dependent. Verify current law before relying on generalized guidance.
